Buttermilk Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • A dash of nutmeg

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and prepare pie crust in the pie plate.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add eggs and flour, mix until smooth.
  4. Add buttermilk, vanilla, and nutmeg. Mix well.
  5. Pour batter into pie crust.
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick can be inserted and come out clean.

Chocolate Chocolate Chip Brownie (Source)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (melted)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square baking pan.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt 1 cup dark chocolate chips on medium power in 30-second intervals until just melted. Set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, beat butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar until creamy. Add melted chocolate and mix until combined.
  5. Add egg and vanilla extract and mix to combine well.
  6. Add the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt mix until blended
  7. Stir in remaining cup of chocolate chips.
  8. Spread the dough into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, give the pan a little shake. If it jiggles in the middle, put the pan back in and try again every 5 minutes until there is no longer any jiggle.
  9. Allow to cool at least 15 minutes before serving.

Peanut Butter Blossoms

Ingredients:

  • Betty Crocker Peanut Butter Cookie Mix
  • Hershey's Kisses Milk Chocolate Candies
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 egg

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine cookie mix, vegetable oil, water, and egg in a mixing bowl.
  3. Take one tablespoon of dough, roll into ball, and place in mini-muffin baking sheet.
  4. Place in oven and bake for 7-8 minutes or until brown on top.
  5. Once baked, take Hershey's kisses and gently push down into cookie.
  6. Allow to cool completely and use a spoon to remove from baking sheet.
  7. Repeat with the remaining dough. Makes around 4 dozen cookies.
